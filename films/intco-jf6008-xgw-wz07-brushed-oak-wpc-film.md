## Technical Notes

- **Substrate:** WPC (Wood Plastic Composite) — superior moisture resistance vs MDF/HDF
- **EIR Quality:** Clearly visible embossed-in-register texture — the best tactile surface in the wood film catalog
- **Installation:** Panel click/adhesive system — different from film wrap workflow
- **Dimensions:** Standard WPC wall panel format (likely 2800×600mm or similar fluted)

---

## Key Insight

JF6008-XGW-WZ07 is the most tactilely authentic wood surface in the catalog — you can see and feel the grain lines, which no flat PVC/PET film can match. The WPC substrate also outperforms film on MDF for monsoon humidity. The limitation is market maturity: wood-grain WPC panels are a newer category in India vs stone-look panels (where INTCO already has traction). Position alongside INTCO's stone panels as the "wood complement" — architects who specify SY08 travertine for one wall may want JF6008 brushed oak for the adjacent wall.

## Priority Fix

Develop a **lighter Japandi variant** (blonde-greige tone, same EIR texture) to capture the Bengaluru/Pune millennial segment. Current warm-brown tone targets mid-market; a light variant could score 7.5–7.8 with the design-forward segment.
