## Film Mimicry Notes

- **Complexity:** Moderate — clean grain is easier to replicate than figured walnut
- **Key Challenge:** Achieving the subtle grey wash over warm blonde without looking flat/dead
- **EIR Requirement:** Medium — fine semi-open pore needs light embossing for premium feel
- **Pattern Repeat:** 2.5m+ recommended for large wardrobes/walls
- **Pitfall:** Over-saturating the warm tone — must stay in grey-blonde territory, not shift to honey-amber
