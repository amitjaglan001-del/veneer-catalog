## Visual Profile

- **Base Tone:** Medium chocolate-brown — classic J. regia warm walnut. Neither too dark (espresso) nor too light (caramel). Dead-centre walnut sweet spot for India
- **Grain Pattern:** Crown/cathedral cut with flowing curved arches — the most character-rich cut in walnut. Multiple arch centres create natural rhythm
- **Tonal Variation:** Good — lighter zones between cathedral arches, darker along grain lines. Natural-looking depth
- **Figure:** Strong cathedral arch figure with 2–3 visible focal arch centres. Flowing curves add visual movement
- **Pore:** Semi-open, medium grain. No visible EIR embossing — surface appears flat/smooth despite grain print
- **Character:** Classic, warm, characterful — reads as real walnut slab at arm's length

## Film Mimicry Notes

- **Complexity:** Moderate — cathedral arch requires accurate curvature rendering
- **EIR Requirement:** High — cathedral walnut demands pore embossing to avoid "plastic" read at close range
- **Key Challenge:** Getting the arch curvature natural (not symmetrical/mechanical) and achieving tonal depth between arches
- **Pattern Repeat:** 2.0m+ minimum; cathedral arches repeat is noticeable on wide walls
- **Pitfall:** Over-regular arch spacing; too-warm (orange) tone shift; flat surface betraying film nature

## Key Insight

WN1508-6 lands in the highest-volume intersection of Indian premium taste: cathedral walnut, chocolate-brown, warm satin. It will sell across 8 out of 10 city markets and 3 out of 4 consumer segments. The ceiling is set by flat film surface (no EIR) and absence of heartwood-sapwood contrast — both fixable. With the stain/scratch resistant treatment, this film has a practical durability story that most walnut films lack. Position as the "everyday premium walnut" — reliable, warm, characterful.

## Priority Fix

Request **EIR open-pore matte variant** (~8–12% sheen) with light heartwood-sapwood cream band. Would push mimicry from 6.9 to ~7.5 and overall score to ~8.1–8.3. The cathedral figure + EIR combination would be the strongest walnut film in the catalog.
