## Visual Profile

- **Base Tone:** Cool greige — grey-beige with slight warm undertone; lighter than SY130 but cooler than SY134
- **Banding:** Horizontal layered banding typical of cross-cut travertine; darker grey-brown streaks concentrate on left third
- **Pitting:** Visible open pits (洞 = caves/holes) across surface — the defining feature; authentic cross-cut travertine character
- **Veining:** Subtle horizontal bands rather than dramatic veins; low-moderate drama
- **Overall Character:** Geological, tactile, matte — reads as honest natural stone rather than polished luxury

## Key Insight

DXC010-5 is a genuine cave-stone (洞石) travertine with authentic pitting that most smooth-surface stone panels cannot match. The tactile quality is its strongest asset (D6: 4.5/5), making it specification-architect ready for biophilic and wabi-sabi briefs in Bengaluru and Mumbai. However, the cool greige tone and pitted texture limit mass-market appeal — Tier-2 buyers and heritage segments will not recognise the premium intent. Position as a design-channel SKU alongside stone-007, not as a volume driver.

Develop a **warm cream variant** of this pitting texture — combine the SY134 warm-cream tone with DXC010-5's cave-stone surface treatment. Projected score: 78–82 (warm tone lifts D2 to 4.5/5, broadens segment appeal dramatically).
