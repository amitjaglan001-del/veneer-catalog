## India Collection Essentials (Reference Baseline)
Any complete India-facing stone panel collection needs these 5 core SKUs:
1. **Classic White + Grey Vein** (Carrara-style) — most in-demand SKU; high gloss + semi-gloss
2. **Bold White + Gold/Grey Vein** (Calacatta-style) — premium aspiration; bold veining; high gloss
3. **Light Grey Marble** (Grigio-style) — urban modern; tech demographic; semi-gloss or matte
4. **Warm Beige / Cream** (Crema Marfil / Katni-style) — North India + Gujarat + Tier-2; high gloss
5. **Dark / Charcoal Stone** (Nero Marquina / Black Galaxy-style) — accent; drama; high gloss
